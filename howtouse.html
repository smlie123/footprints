FootPrints User Guide

Track your digital journey, one step at a time.

Collecting Footprints

Text Highlights

Select any text on a web page. A floating toolbar will appear allowing you to highlight the text with different styles (Solid, Line, Dash, Wavy) and colors.

Images

There are two ways to save images:

  • Right-click on any image and select "Add to Footprints".
  • Open the Sidepanel, hover over the Camera icon, and click it to capture a screenshot of the visible area.

Quick Notes

Open the Sidepanel (click the extension icon) to jot down quick thoughts related to the current page in the input box.

Reviewing Your Footprints

Sidepanel

The Sidepanel shows you "Footprints on This Page" so you can quickly review your notes for the current URL.

Home Page

Click the "All footprints" home icon in the Sidepanel to open the full dashboard.

  • Timeline View: See your footprints organized chronologically.
  • Masonry View: A card-style layout for browsing.
  • Roaming Mode: Rediscover a random footprint from your past to spark new inspiration.
  • Calendar: Navigate through your history by date.
  • Search: Find specific notes by keywords.

Managing & Annotating

Annotations

In the Home Page, click the Edit icon on any footprint to add personal annotations. You can see a preview of the context (text quote or image) while writing your note.

Locate Context

Click the Location icon on a footprint to open the original page and scroll directly to the highlighted text.

Settings

Customize your highlight styles and colors in the Settings menu (accessible from the Sidepanel or Home Page sidebar).
